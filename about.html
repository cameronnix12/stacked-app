The problem

Most students apply to 20, 50, sometimes 100+ internships. They track everything in a Google Sheet that becomes impossible to maintain. They send the same resume to every job. They forget to follow up. They miss deadlines.

The tools that exist are either built for professionals with 10 years of experience, or they're just prettier spreadsheets. Nothing was built specifically for students navigating their first real job search.

What we built

Stacked is a focused tool that does three things well: tracks your applications, tailors your resume with AI for each job, and generates cover letters that don't sound like they were written by a robot.

We use Claude — Anthropic's AI — because it's the best at following specific instructions. When we tell it to never fabricate skills or experiences, it actually listens. Your resume stays honest. Your cover letter sounds like you.

What we believe

Honesty over inflation

The AI will never invent experience you don't have. A shorter honest resume beats a longer dishonest one every time.
